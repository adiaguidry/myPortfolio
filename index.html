CURRENT PROJECT

BUILT WITH ANGULARJS

GOALS PAY OFF

This is an on going project that I am currently pouring myself into, and I am enjoying every minute I get to spend with it. My final project was inspired by my six year old son, Noah. I wanted to build something with my newly acqired knowledge of AngularJS. Building this project would help me to build on what I had learned. My project is intended to teach Noah how to set goals for himself. I also wanted to teach him the value of a dollar. I thought it would be awesome to combine the two. Understanding the use of Angular two-way data binding, I could see how the benefits would be great for this project. As goals are established by Noah, I can see those goals in the Admin interface, and they are also listed for him to see on his interface. As he completes each goal, he hits a button and I am notified. I can then decide how much he is paid for a job well-done. He is paid in virtual money. The money can be allocated into accounts that are set up in admin interface. Which accounts receive what, however, are determined by Noah as he drags and drops it to the account of his choice.
